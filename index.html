Serena Milesi

I’m a Communication Design student from Politecnico di Milano (Italy) and I recently took part in an exchange program at Plymouth University. My interests span from interactive art, light design, live music and pretty everything design related.

As an incredibly curious person, I’m always seeking new knowledge by surrounding myself with inspiring people and visiting creative places, as well as trying to combine my passions in what I do exploring pathways emerging from intersections between visual art and emerging technologies.

I also love going on adventure discovering the wild side of the nature and wandering around the world!

Source Code Pro - Specimen

This booklet shows the main uses, shapes and anatomy of the typeface Source Code Pro, a monospaced sans serif typeface designed in 2012 by Paul D. Hunt for Adobe Systems. A specimen is a tool for type foundries and type designers used and designed for a commercial purpose or to describe the design process behind a typeface.

Book app trailer

Book app trailer based on Fausto Gilberti’s picture book “L’orco che mangiava i bambini”- Corraini Edizioni.

This educational app is designed to open up the possibilities of creative play within story telling. In particular we worked on transmedia translations by developing a communicative artifact starting from a paper picture book in order to amplify the potential interaction that was already in the book. To start your adventure you have to choose your own character, then you have to play different mini games to escape from the ogre, can you get to the end without ending up in the ogre’s larder?

Picture rights reserved to the artist Fausto Gilberti.
